inWorkFlow is a system of management of the company. Due to our system you will always be in work flow making effective monitoring and managing your working environment
From now setting tasks, making appointments, pointing calendar events or just chatting with the staff using inWorkFlow becomes easier
System features
Managing projects
Using WorkFlow tools you can manage your projects easier than ever. Setting tasks to the stuff and controlling terms of their completing, discussing various issues of the project, managing the resources - all this becomes possible from anywhere in the world where there is Internet access.
Corporate events
You can plan variety of activities like corporate meetings, celebrating birthdays etc. The system will automatically send invitations to all the participants and remind of the incoming event.
Customer access
You can give a customer access to one or more projects to organize the approach of the customer with the performer directly that greatly saves time while managing the task.
Stuff communication
All your employees will be able to communicate in informal atmosphere using corporate social network, and for work tasks use an easy and convenient corporate chat.
Flexible reports
Users will be able to customize reports. You decide by yourself what will be appeared in the reports.
Permission levels
In the WorkFlow system is presented flexible configuration of permission levels. You will be able to configure personal user's rights and rights of the entire company department.
Why users choose us?

To configure the system you do not need any special knowledge. You can configure the system without any help of others. Setup Wizard allows you to configure the system in a few minutes.
User frendly interface
The system provides an easy and user frendly interface. You don't have to spend much time studying the system. After registration you can immediately start working.
Windows Client
After installing inWorkFlow Client on your computer you'll always be up to date with the latest company events. Also the inWorkFlow Client has an imbedded messenger that will make stuff communication as comfortable as ever.
Mobile interface
With our mobile interface you will be able to stay online wherever you are. Outdoors or indoors, at work or at home inWorkFlow is always with you.
